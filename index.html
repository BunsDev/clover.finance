Clover is a Polkadot-built DeFi portal and service provider that offers a series of innovative products and services. Through putting together an ecosystem that gathers users, community developers and upper-layer applications, Clover will foster an all-in-one open DeFi platform that allows for more possibilities.

DEFI SERVICE PROVIDER

Clover’s already-integrated DeFi protocol library, allows a lego-like building environment that will significantly reduce the time and technical threshold for the development of upper-layer applications. Clover will foster an enhanced development environment that unleashes the full potential of decentralized finance.

Trading Protocol

Lending Protocol
