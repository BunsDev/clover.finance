    Clover Services

    Swap Protocol
    Trade directly from the user’s wallet without going through a third party.
    Wrapper Protocol
    Trade any assets between different chains instantly.
    Vault Protocol
    Receive tradeable staking rewards, unlocking the derivative value of staked assets.
    Lending Protocol
    Deposit assets to earn interest or borrow.
    Stablecoin Protocol
    Support native stablecoin issuance.
    Governance Application
    Allow users to implement or make changes to modules.

    Why Clover?

    Scalable
    Clover can scale seamlessly as a parachain of Polkadot by allowing specialized blockchains to communicate with each other in a secure and trustless environment.
    Cross-chain
    Cross-chain transactions are made available through high interoperability between different parachain assets. Interoperability is ensured by Polkadot’s cross-chain message passing technology.
    Sustainable
    The elimination of high gas fees and network congestion will allow instant settlement and lower transaction fee.
    Trustless & Secure
    A non-custodial, decentralized P2P trading platform that allows only the account holders to access funds.
    Private
    Trade transactions anonymously without providing any personal information.
    Open Platform
    Developers are encouraged to contribute to modules and applications. The community can propose, vote, and implement changes through decentralized governance modules.
