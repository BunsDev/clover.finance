Development
Clover Kernel and User Interface are developed by first-party developers.
Functions
Clover High Level Smart Contract, through Tulip technology, realizes one-stop development and cross-chain deployment of Smart Contract.
Tulip language, basic development tools, and development documents are implemented by first-party developers.
SC developers, based on Clover development tools, develop a variety of dApps with a lower cost.
One-click deployment reduces the deployment cost of rented network, storage, and bandwidth.
Development
Tulip and development tools are developed by first-party developers.
dApps are developed and operated by third-party developers.
Functions

What does Kernel contain ?

Rose - Universal Crypto Signer
responsible for supporting cross-chain signatures.
Tulip - Higher Level Smart Contract Language
responsible for one-stop development and cross-chain deployment of Smart Contract.
Skura - H5 to Native Adapter
responsible for the adaptation of front-end H5 and native language.
Orchid - Deep Learning Indexing System
responsible for indexing, querying and recommending dApps through deep learning.
